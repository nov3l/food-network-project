Most Traditional Foods of Bangladesh

Bangladesh has got some very tasty traditional dishes. These are mouthwatreing & yummy.
Below you can see 3 of our most famous foods in Bangladesh you must try.

Mejban

Mejban is a Persian word literally meaning a host, The word now means community feasting, a tradition that organized in Chattogram region. It is usually host by the rich people and the occasion could be like, birthday, death anniversary, observance day, inauguration of a new business etc.

Kacci Biriyani

Kacchi means 'raw' - When the meat is added raw to the biryani pot, then it is referred to as Kacchi Biryani whereas Pakki is when the completely or partially cooked meat is added to the biryani pot and is sometimes layered.

Fried Fish

A fish fry is a meal containing battered or breaded fried fish. It typically also includes french fries, coleslaw, hushpuppies, lemon slices, tartar sauce, hot sauce, malt vinegar and dessert. Some Native American versions are cooked by coating fish with semolina and egg yolk.
